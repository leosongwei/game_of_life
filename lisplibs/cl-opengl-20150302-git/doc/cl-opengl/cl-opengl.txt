1 Introduction
**************

CL-OPENGL is a set of CFFI bindings to OPENGL 2.0.  GLU and FreeGLUT
bindings are also included.

   Notice that the GLUT bindings are mostly provided for the purpose of
supporting the examples included in the distribution and you are free to
use another windowing toolkit.
